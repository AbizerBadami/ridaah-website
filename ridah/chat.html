Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom Rida take?

Custom orders typically take 10–14 working days from confirmation, depending on fabric availability and complexity.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Ready-to-wear items can be exchanged within 7 days of delivery. Custom orders are non-returnable as they are made to your measurements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI payments via QR code (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM). Bank transfer is available for orders above ₹5,000.

Do you ship outside India?

Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping charges and timelines vary by destination. Contact us on WhatsApp for a quote.
